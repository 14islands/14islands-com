Pushing the limits of Shopify.

Oyyo is a producer of hand woven dhurries, uniquely made from 100% organic cotton and vivid vegetable dye. They wanted a new online store to match the quality of their N°2 collection.

Getting organic

We built them a fully responsive online store using the Shopify platform. The store features design and interactions to present their products in the nicest possible way.

Design

The site was designed by our friends at Studio Källbom, a talented design studio with an artistic touch.

The process

Oyyo produces their products with care, the same applied for the website. The team collaborated on Slack throughout the whole process. We set the bar high and pushed the limits of Shopify to get the experience right.

New identity brought to life.

Scandinavian Property Group (SPG) is a real estate company specialised in the Scandinavian market, with offices both in Oslo, Norway and Stockholm, Sweden. They wanted a fresh new website as part of a new company identity.

Enter the Experience

The idea with the website is to present SPG and their real estate portfolio in a clear and inspiring way. Simple structure, interactive elements and smooth transitions between pages make the user experience seamless.

The website supports multiple languages and is currently available in Swedish (spg.se) and Norwegian (spg.no). It’s made with WordPress so its fully editable by the people at SPG, and its built responsive to work well on mobile, tablets and desktop computers.

Design

BVD designed the identity and website. BVD is an experienced design studio in Stockholm that focuses on simplicity and quality.

Injecting the spirit

In addition to crafting a fully responsive experience, the goal was to inspire as you scroll through the pages. We built simple animations and transitions to enhance the experience.

We built a custom designed map that allows users to zoom closer to locations. Each level of the map exposes different level of details.

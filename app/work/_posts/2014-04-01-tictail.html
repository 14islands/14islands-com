{% markdown %} ## About [Tictail](http://www.tictail.com/) is a Stockholm-based startup that enables people with great products to easily sell online. They wanted a modern website for the Tictail brand, increase signups and to help their community to succeed. The site is fully responsive and built in Wordpress to make all content editable by the people at Tictail. {% endmarkdown %}
{% include casestudy-responsive.html url="/images/work/tictail/iphone6screenshot.jpg" alt="Tictail on Mobile" %}
{% markdown %} ## Design The site was designed by our friends at [Studio Källbom](http://www.studiokallbom.se/). Studio Källbom is a talented design studio with an artistic touch. The site features a magazine like design: nice typography, bold colors and rich content to show features and tell Tictails story. We were totally inspired to make this our best work ever. {% endmarkdown %}

{% markdown %} ## Enter the Experience The idea with the website is to present SPG and their real estate portfolio in a clear and inspiring way. Simple structure, interactive elements and smooth transitions between pages make the user experience seamless. The website supports multiple languages and is currently available in Swedish ([sgp.se](http://sgp.se)) and Norwegian ([spg.no](http://sgp.no)). Its made with WordPress so its fully editable by the people at SPG, and its built responsive to work well on mobile, tablets and desktop computers. {% endmarkdown %}

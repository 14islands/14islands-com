{% markdown %} ## Decorations The overall experience was directed by our friends at [UPPERQUAD](http://upperquad.com) this year and [UENO](http://ueno.co) last year. The design features colourful flat elements such as moving animals and elves in different colour outfits. All design elements are vector based - a good approach to keep things flexible and resolution independent. As always, we made everything work on all kinds of devices, this year we even added support for Chromecast and Android Wear integration. {% endmarkdown %}
{% include wide-content-image.html src="/images/work/google-santa-tracker/santa-tracker-playground-desktop.png" alt="The playground scene features many fun animations and eyes that follow the mouse." ratio="16_10" margin="both" %}
{% markdown %} ## Bringing it to life Apart from coding the colourful characters and their surroundings, our job was to bring the whole experience to life. Animations and interactions should feel natural and work nicely together to make scenes playful and as fun as possible. {% endmarkdown %}
{% include work/google-santa-tracker/reindeer-animations.html %}
{% markdown %} Many of the scenes were fully fledged games, such as the matching game where you play against the clock to beat santa on his own playfield. We built the logic for these games with multiple levels and score keeping. {% endmarkdown %}
{% include wide-content-image.html src="/images/work/google-santa-tracker/santa-tracker-matching-game-desktop.png" alt="Santa Tracker Matching Game" ratio="16_10" margin="both" %}
{% markdown %} We also integrated with many cool Google services such as the Street View Panorama and the Text-to-Speech API. The translations scene lets you listen to your favourite holiday greetings in 20 different languages - including Elvish. {% endmarkdown %}
{% include wide-content-image.html src="/images/work/google-santa-tracker/santa-tracker-translations-desktop.png" alt="Santa Translation scene" ratio="16_10" margin="both" %}
{% markdown %} Our office buddies [Plan8](http://plan8.se/) did an amazing job with the sound design for the whole project. {% endmarkdown %}
{% markdown %} ## Technologies As always, when working with Google, we got to play with a lot of new toys. This year Santa's factory was upgraded to use Web Components (using Polymer) and the new Web Animations specification. {% endmarkdown %}

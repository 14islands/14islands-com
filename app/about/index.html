We are
14islands.

We are a web development studio that cares deeply about creating great user experiences. 14islands was founded in 2011, though our roots in the web industry stretch back more than 15 years.

{% include wide-content-image.html src="../images/about/we-are-14islands.jpg" alt="The 14islands HQ" ratio="3_2" margin="both" %}

We care
about the web.

Digital experiences need many ingredients to make your users happy. Good design. Good performance. Responsive for all devices and screen sizes. Natural and playful interactions. Continual improvement based on usage data. This is the work we do.

{% include wide-content-image.html src="../images/about/we-care.jpg" alt="Hjörtur and Marco on a hangout at the 14islands office" ratio="3_2" margin="both" caption=true shapeClass="shape-overlay--bottom-right" shapeSVG="about-image-bottom-right.svg" %}

We love
what we do.

There’s always something cooking in the 14islands kitchen. We are constantly experimenting with emerging technologies, and never miss an opportunity to give talks or workshops on web development.

{% include wide-content-image.html src="../images/about/we-love-what-we-do.jpg" alt="Marco giving a Responsive Web Development workshop." ratio="16_9" margin="both" caption=true shapeClass="shape-overlay--top-left" shapeSVG="about-image-top-left.svg" %}

The 14islands blog is our main channel for sharing our latest findings and clever techniques. We update our Codepen frequently with inspiring animations or small snippets of code. If we think something will benefit the community, we put it on our Github page.
